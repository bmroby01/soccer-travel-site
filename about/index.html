Meet the AO Adventurers

Brian Davis

Brian is the founder of AO Adventures and an original host of the BarrelProof Podcast.

Brian is a husband and father who is very passionate about soccer. He has been to over 50 U.S. Men's and Women's National Team games all around the world. In his spare time he is a co-host of the BarrelProof Podcast.
